THE POWER OF UBUNTU MEETS THE BEauty OF GNOME desktop.

Ubuntu GNOME is an official flavor of Ubuntu, featuring the GNOME desktop environment. Ubuntu GNOME is a mostly pure GNOME desktop experience built from the Ubuntu repositories. Three years ago, Ubuntu GNOME started as an unofficial flavor of Ubuntu - see the release notes of 12.10 - and 6 months after that, Ubuntu GNOME has become an official flavor.

Fast forward to today, and we are proud to announce the release of 15.10. This is our Non-LTS Release. If you seek stability and long support, please consider Ubuntu GNOME 14.04 (Trusty Tahr) LTS Release. If you want the latest software/packages that we can offer, then give Ubuntu GNOME 15.04 (Wily Werewolf) a try today.

15.10 hotfix for GRUB installations has been released. See the change log on GitHub for details.

Lightweight.

Ubuntu GNOME runs on the GNOME SHell, a fast and lightweight desktop environment. It runs great on a variety of hardware, and the minimum requirements include only a 1GHz processor and 1.5GB of RAM.

Powerful.

It also comes with all the default packages of Ubuntu. Whether you need to do software development, word processing, graphics work, or simply browse the web, there is plenty of free software to go around.

Open source.

Best of all, the entire Ubuntu GNOME project is open source and entirely community driven. Want to see a change made in the way it works? All of the code is publicly available and ready to be modified.

A Beautiful desktop.

Every part of GNOME 3 has been designed to make it simple and easy to use. The Activities Overview is an easy way to access all your basic tasks. A press of a button is all it takes to view your open windows, launch applications or check if you have new messages. Having everything in one place is convenient and means that you don’t have to learn your way around a maze of different technologies.

Plus with Tweak Tool, you’ve got full customization over everything inside of GNOME. You can change themes, colors, even scaling for hi-res displays. You can also download community made extensions or create your own to add additional features and functionality to your desktop environment.

Free office apps for all.

Ubuntu GNOME comes pre-installed with LibreOffice 5, a powerful office suite; Its clean interface and its powerful tools let you unleash your creativity and grow your productivity. Gone are the days of needing to pay for your office suite, as LibreOffice is completely free, to use forever.

LibreOffice embeds several applications that make it the most powerful Free & Open Source Office suite on the market. Process text documents, create and edit spreadsheets and database files, make presentations, even edit images. Best of all, its completely free, and even works with existing files from other office suites. PDF, DOC, XLSX, CSV, & PPT are all compatible.

Music, movies, games & more.

Wily Werewolf was made  for media lovers. On top of having Flash and HTML5 web browser capabilities for streaming content online, the latest version of Ubuntu GNOME comes with VLC media player for viewing any format of video you can throw at it, Rythmbox music player for listening to your favorite tunes, and Krita for editing and viewing images. DLNA media streaming is also built right into the OS, allowing you to access other media servers on the network or create your own.

Plus, thanks to Steam, there are more games than ever before available for Ubuntu GNOME. Over 1,000 Linux-compatible games are ready to be played using Keyboard + Mouse, or your favorite controller. Games range from triple A to indie, and span across any genre imaginable.

The best of the web.

15.10 comes with both Mozilla Firefox and Google Chromium installed, so you are free to browse the web your way. All of your desktop applications can also be linked to, and sync with your online accounts. Web browsing on Ubuntu GNOME is fast and secure, with native VPN support on an operating system level.

Are you a web developer or software engineer? Ubuntu GNOME comes with the best developer tools for those who help build the web. Git & SVN are pre-configured, and the terminal is always accessible with just a keystroke. Additional text-editors and IDE’s are also just a download away from the Ubuntu Software Center.

Do more with gnome software!

GNOME Software lets you browse and install thousands of free and paid applications available for Ubuntu. You can view available software by category, or search quickly by name or description. You can also view the software packages already installed, and remove items you no longer need. GNOME Software keeps all of your packages up to date, and makes software and operating system upgrades a breeze.

You can even expand the software center by adding your own custom and community managed repositories. Best of all, the vast majority of software is completely free and open source.

Why ubuntu?

Ubuntu is a Debian-based Linux operating system and distribution for personal computers, smartphones, and network servers. It's based on free software and named after the Southern African philosphy of ubuntu (literally, "human-ness"), which often is translated as "humanity towards humanity." Ubuntu is backed by Canonical, making it the most commercially supported distribution of Linux on the planet.

Why GNOME?

GNOME is an easy and elegant way to use youc computer. It is designed to put you in control and bring freedom to everybody. GNOME 3 is developed by the GNOME community, a diverse international group of contributors that is supported by an independent, non-profit foundation. It began as the official desktop environment for Debian.

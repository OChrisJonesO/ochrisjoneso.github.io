A bit more about myself...

Being a graphic designer and a developer is the perfect one-two punch, as not only can I visualize a beautiful new website, application, or other user experience, I also have the technical know-how to build it. From the first time I ever laid hands on a computer, I’ve had a growing obsession for technology. I grew up taking apart and fixing PCs, playing tons of retro games, and always trying to learn more. Fast forward to today, and not much has changed. I have more computers and servers in my apartment than I can count, develop video games in my free time, bury myself in code, stay up way later than I probably should, and am constantly teaching myself new things. I’ve taken my passion for computer hardware and software and combined it with my love of creating. I work diligently to grow my skill set each and every day as a content creator, and love working with experimental technologies in virtual/augmented reality applications. I specialize primarily in web, mobile, 3D, and motion/video work, but am also comfortable with more traditional print mediums. When I’m not working on a new project, I’m probably hosting LAN parties, getting my drone stuck in a tree, rocking out to some music, or making some delicious vegan food.
